Mental Models

  • Not complex
  • An map (or picture, or story)
  • Contradicted or confirmed
First concept is Mental Models

In order to successfully use an interface, users must come to understand the rules of that interface.

Users construct a mental model of how that system works. Does not match with the complex, engineered model of how that system actually works.

They create a picture (or a map, or a story) that tells them what is happening, and what is going to happen
